3D Modeling - Surfaces

Terrain, pipelines, structures – visualised before they exist.

I create and edit 3D surface models for infrastructure, water and civil engineering projects. From terrain models and pipe networks to complex river and reservoir systems – these models form the basis for option studies, quantity take-offs, BIM workflows and visualisations.

My services include:

  • 3D surface models (surfaces) for terrain, alignments and structures
  • Integration with and into 3D solid models
  • Network-based pipeline models
  • 2D derivations with dimensions, cross-sections and longitudinal profiles
  • Quantity surveying and billing-relevant evaluations

My background in hydraulic engineering, concrete construction, civil engineering, pipeline construction and municipal infrastructure planning enables precise, practice-oriented implementation – even with large data volumes and heterogeneous planning environments.

BIM in earthworks: I actively integrate civil engineering projects into BIM structures. Solutions are evolving rapidly – and already today BIM elements can be used meaningfully and efficiently in earthworks.

Core services

  • Creation and editing of 3D terrain and surface models
  • Option studies, volume calculations, quantity take-offs and billing
  • Integration of surface, solid and BIM models
  • Generation of cross-sections, longitudinal sections and planning documents
  • Processing of legacy and survey data (point clouds, DTMs)
  • Integration of earthworks and civil engineering projects into BIM structures

Software

I currently work primarily with AutoCAD Civil 3D.
My experience also includes:
Aquaveo SMS (Surface-water Modeling System), BricsCAD, ArcGIS, QGIS, Global Mapper, and photogrammetry software such as Agisoft Metashape.

Upon request, projects can also be processed in these systems or their current versions (ArcGIS Pro, BricsCAD Civil, Global Mapper Pro, Agisoft Metashape Professional).
