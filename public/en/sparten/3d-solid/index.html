3D Modeling - Solids

From concept to form – precise, versatile, combinable.

I create highly detailed 3D solid models for technical, structural and plant-specific applications – exactly to the level of detail your project requires.

Source materials include:

  • Sketches and descriptions
  • Point clouds
  • 2D drawings
  • Your ideas discussed at the table – I help turn thoughts into digital form

Further processing & integration:

  • Integration into BIM workflows and 3D terrain models
  • 2D derivations with dimensions and manufacturing-relevant information
  • Generation of bills of quantities
  • Visualisations for planning, execution and communication

This results in consistent models that combine technical precision with spatial, topographical and structural information.

Core services

  • 3D modelling of components, assemblies and plants
  • Visualisation of technical systems and components
  • Combination of solid and surface models within BIM workflows
  • Derivation of 2D drawings and manufacturing documentation
  • Integration into bills of quantities and planning processes
  • Processing of point clouds, sketches and legacy data

Software

I currently work primarily with AutoCAD.
Blender is also used for format conversion and certain modelling steps.

My experience further includes:
Autodesk Plant 3D, Revit, PTC Pro/ENGINEER, CATIA V5 (and to a small extent V4), and Siemens NX.

Upon request, work can also be carried out in these systems or their current versions (Creo Parametric, CATIA V6/3DEXPERIENCE, Siemens NX Series).
Extensive experience with Blender is also available.
