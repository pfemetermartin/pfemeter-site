# Technical infrastructure requires clear planning.

I support public and private clients in the **planning and implementation of technical infrastructure projects** – from initial concepts and feasibility studies through planning and modelling across all relevant project phases, including the preparation of bills of quantities and tender documentation, to project preparation and the management or support of site supervision during invoice verification, review of variation claims and schedule management. My focus lies on projects in the fields of **water management, infrastructure and technical installations as well as river engineering and flood protection**, particularly involving pipeline networks, technical structures and complex interfaces between planning, modelling and construction.

Through my experience in planning, modelling and project support, I combine **technical detail with a systematic view of the overall project**. Precise models, clear planning foundations and structured decision processes create the basis for well-founded decisions and efficient project execution.

Clients benefit from my experience in construction works in both urban and rural environments, including works within public roads under live traffic as well as modifications carried out during ongoing operations. I am also familiar with the required permits, official approvals and traffic management permissions as well as the organisational requirements of construction execution.

## Permitting, structural design and geotechnics

Within my engineering activities, I also prepare technical documentation for approval procedures and address structural and geotechnical questions related to technical infrastructure. This includes in particular water law permitting documents, structural design of pipeline infrastructure as well as excavation and geotechnical concepts for infrastructure and technical installation projects. All services are provided within the professional scope of engineering activities in the fields of water management, infrastructure and technical installations.

## My project experience includes:

- Water management facilities and infrastructure structures  
- Pipeline networks for water, sewer and technical utilities  
- Infrastructure projects in urban environments  
- 3D modelling of technical structures and installations  
- Planning and modelling of complex piping and pipeline systems  

I work both on **conceptual planning tasks** and on the **precise technical implementation in models and engineering drawings**. This allows planning alternatives to be evaluated at an early stage, interfaces to be clarified and decisions to be prepared in a transparent and structured way.

**My focus:** Clear planning foundations, precise models and structured project work that connects technical requirements, planning and construction.
