# Technical infrastructure requires clear planning.

I support public and private clients in the **planning and implementation of technical infrastructure projects** – from initial concepts and feasibility studies through planning and modelling across all relevant project phases, including the preparation of bills of quantities and tender documents, to project preparation for construction as well as leading or supporting site supervision in invoice verification, evaluation of change orders and schedule management. My main focus lies in projects within **water management, infrastructure and plant engineering as well as river engineering and flood protection**, particularly involving utility networks, technical structures and complex interfaces between planning, modelling and execution.

Through my experience in planning, modelling and project support, I combine **technical detail with a systematic view of the overall project**. Precise models, clear planning foundations and structured decision processes provide the basis for well-informed decisions and efficient project workflows.

Clients benefit from my experience in construction environments in both **urban and non-urban settings**, including work within public streets under ongoing traffic as well as **modifications during ongoing operations**. I am also familiar with the required permits, approvals and traffic-related authorisations as well as the organisational requirements of construction management.

## My project experience includes:
- Water management facilities and infrastructure structures  
- Utility networks for water, sewer and technical media  
- Infrastructure projects in urban environments  
- 3D modelling of technical structures and facilities  
- Planning and modelling of complex pipe and utility systems  

I work on both **conceptual planning tasks** and the **precise technical implementation in models and technical documentation**. This allows planning alternatives to be evaluated at an early stage, interfaces to be clarified and decisions to be prepared in a transparent and traceable way.

**My focus:** Clear planning foundations, precise models and structured project work that connects technical requirements, planning and implementation.
